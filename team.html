Our Team of Experts

We like to think of ourselves as a small but perfectly formed lettings & management agency. Working you get the exposure, knowledge and expertise you would expect from a large agent, but the service you will only receive from a smaller business built around 100% client and tenant focus.

Rizwan Chaudhry CBI | CFC | CIPS

Business & Real Estate Advisor

Rizwan is a serial entrepreneur and a trailblazer with innovative ideas and purest form of belief in them. For the last 20 years he has been engaged in commercially viable ventures encompassing commercial real estate, real estate development projects, franchising and business sales & purchase sectors. He has been with Ghories since 2018 and focus on business planning & Investment Appraisals.

Rizwan is a Certified International Property Specialist (CIPS), Certified Business Intermediary (CBI), Certified Franchise Consultant (CFC), Certified Commercial Investment Member (CCIM) Candidate, and a licensed realtor with Fraser Valley Board, British Columbia, Canada. Rizwan holds degree of Masters in Business Administration
